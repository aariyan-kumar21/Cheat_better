
# Cheat Better

**Overview**

EduLearn is a frontend-based educational platform built during the "Cheat Better" college event. The goal of this project is to simplify the way students learn by bringing notes, video lectures, and quizzes together in one place.

Students often struggle to find well-structured notes while having access to thousands of video lectures. EduNotes solves this problem by providing a centralized platform where learning resources are easy to access, share, and use.


**Problem Statement**  

- Students can easily find video lectures online
- But finding concise, quality notes is difficult
- Switching between multiple platforms wastes time

**Solution**

EduNotes provides an all-in-one learning platform where students can:

📖 Read well-organized notes  
⬆️ Upload and share their own notes  
🎥 Watch video lectures  
🧠 Take quizzes based on the content
